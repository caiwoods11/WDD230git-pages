Description of the Audience

The Audience of Get Climate is a metropolitan locals or visitor coming to the city wanting to explore. Get Climate is for introverts and extroverts like Yelp is for Foodies. In addition to the weather climate, it shows the social enviroment of a location. Vistors may want to hit the hottest most bumpin places, where as a family may want to stay away from the crowds.
